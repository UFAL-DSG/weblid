How to start recording?

To start recording your own voice or any sound, press the "Push to speak" button. When enough voice audio is recorded, press the "Stop" button.

What does mean "Did I get that right?"

When the recording stops, you can give us feedback about whether guessed the sound of the language correctly. Simple answers "Yes" and "No" are usually good enough. It would be grate if you can provide correct sound or language label when we get it wrong.

How do I choose a sound or language label?

Just type in some letters from the label and choose the correct label from the options that have appeared.
