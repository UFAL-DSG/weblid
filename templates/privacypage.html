The use of microphone

This application use your microphone to record sounds around you or your voice. The recording is used to recognise the type of the sound or a language you have spoken. The recording starts when you pres the "Push to speak" button and ends when you press "Stop" button.

What happens with the recorded sounds and voice?

The recorded sound and voice is stored on our servers. We retain the right to use the data for for research and commercial purposes. The recordings can be provided to third parties. Please do not record anything you do not want anyone to know about.

The use of geo-location

Your geo-position is used in verifying your responses. The geo-position is again stored on our servers. We retain the right to use the data for for research and commercial purposes. The recordings can be provided to third parties.

For what are you going to use the collected data?

The sound and voice recordings will be primarily used to improve our dialogue systems such as ALEX.
